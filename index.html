Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I add extra services?

    Yes, you absolutely can add extra services. For example, if you want to add a clay bar, polish & wax application to your Premium Wash service, we can acomplish that. Pricing with be adjusted accordingly.

  • Are your services priced at a flat rate?

    No, our prices are set at a starting base rate for small sedans, crossovers, and trucks. For medium size, XL vehicles, exotics, and RV's our pricing will vary.

  • Do you provide ceramic coatings?

    We currently do not provide liquid ceramic coatings. However, we do have ceramic waxes & ceramic polishing products that have the same ceramic coating characteristics.

  • Can you come to my place of work?

    Absolutely! We have gone to clients' place of work to service their vehicle as long as we are able to be on the premises.

  • Do you have physical location?

    Yes, we do have a physical location available for Premium Washes only. We are at the Van Buren Drive-In Swapmeet Tuesdays & Thursdays 7 am-12 pm. Discount offers available at physical location!
